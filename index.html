How to use this tool

5 steps to a complete answer

1
Set the mode
Choose whether your question has a character (person/animal) or not (nature, poem, abstract). Then choose if there is a transformation (before → after change).
2
Fill 4 details
Enter the stem situation (what the CQ uddiapak describes), the book/poem name, the character or subject, and a rich theme phrase (2–5 words, not 1 word).
3
Read Part C or D
Switch to the Part C or Part D tab to see your answer built sentence by sentence. Orange = upgraded sentence. Green = optional insert.
4
Copy the answer
Go to the Live Answer tab, pick Part C or D, and press Copy answer. Paste directly into your answer sheet or digital document.
5
Add depth (optional)
Visit the Depth phrases tab for 5 universal bonus sentences — tap any to copy it. Insert between sentences 9–10 to make your answer stand out.
